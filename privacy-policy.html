Beads

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Beads ("the app," "we," "our") is developed by PAX Y.LIGER. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

The short version

Beads has no server of its own. Everything you do in the app — practice check-ins, journal entries, mood tags, and settings — is stored either only on your device, or in your own private iCloud account. We (the developer) never see it, store it elsewhere, or share it with anyone.

What data the app handles

  • Practice history — the dates you mark a daily practice complete (including make-up entries).
  • Journal entries — the reflection text and mood tags you choose to save.
  • App settings — your quote language preference, notification on/off state and times, and whether you've subscribed to Beads Pro.

This data is stored:

  1. Locally on your device, in a storage area shared between the app and its Home Screen/Lock Screen widgets.
  2. In your own private CloudKit database, which is part of your personal iCloud account. This lets your data sync across your own devices. Apple's iCloud terms govern that storage; we cannot access it.

No account creation, sign-up, or login is required to use Beads.

Photos

If you subscribe to Beads Pro and choose a custom photo as a Ripple Card background, that photo is read from your photo library and used only on your device to render the card. It is never uploaded anywhere. The resulting card image is only shared or saved if you explicitly tap Share or Save.

Subscriptions and payment

Beads Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details — Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction. See the in-app purchase screen for current pricing and terms.

Notifications

Daily reminder notifications are scheduled entirely on your device using iOS's local notification system. No push notification server is involved, and no data about your notification preferences leaves your device (other than syncing via your own iCloud account, per above).

What we don't do

  • No analytics or usage tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising or ad networks.
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data.
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

Your control over your data

In Settings, you can at any time:

  • Export my data — generates a file containing your practice and journal history.
  • Delete all my data — permanently erases your local and iCloud data from the app.

Deleting the app from your device also removes its local storage; your iCloud data can be removed via the in-app delete option beforehand, or by managing your iCloud storage in iOS Settings.

Children's privacy

Beads is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them any differently than described above (i.e., not at all, beyond the user's own device/iCloud storage).

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly. Material changes will be reflected in an updated App Store listing.

Contact

Questions about this policy: paxliger@icloud.com

中文版

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年7月9日

Beads（以下称"本应用"）由 PAX Y.LIGER 开发。本政策说明本应用处理哪些数据，以及如何处理。

简单来说

Beads 没有自己的服务器。你在应用中的所有操作——打卡记录、日记内容、心情标签、设置——要么只保存在你的设备上，要么保存在你自己的 iCloud 私有数据库中。开发者本人不会看到、存储在别处，或与任何人共享这些数据。

应用处理哪些数据

  • 打卡记录 —— 你标记完成每日练习的日期（含补打卡记录）。
  • 日记内容 —— 你选择保存的反思文字和心情标签。
  • 应用设置 —— 语录语言偏好、通知开关及时间、是否订阅了 Beads Pro。

这些数据存储于：

  1. 设备本地——应用与其主屏幕/锁屏小组件共享的存储区域。
  2. 你自己的 iCloud 私有数据库——属于你个人 iCloud 账户的一部分，用于在你自己的设备间同步数据。该存储受 Apple 的 iCloud 条款约束，我们无法访问。

使用 Beads 无需注册账号或登录。

照片

如果你订阅了 Beads Pro 并为分享卡片选择了自定义照片背景，该照片会从你的相册读取，仅在你的设备本地用于渲染卡片图像，绝不会被上传到任何地方。生成的卡片图像只有在你主动点击"分享"或"保存"时才会被分享或保存。

订阅与支付

Beads Pro 是通过 App Store 由 Apple 全程处理的自动续费订阅。我们不会获取或存储你的支付信息——该交易受 Apple 自身隐私政策约束。具体价格和条款请见应用内购买页面。

通知

每日提醒通知完全由 iOS 本地通知系统在你的设备上调度，不经过任何推送服务器，你的通知偏好数据也不会离开你的设备（除上述通过你自己的 iCloud 账户同步的部分）。

我们不会做的事

  • 不进行任何形式的数据分析或使用追踪。
  • 不展示广告，不接入广告网络。
  • 不集成任何收集数据的第三方 SDK。
  • 不出售、出租或与任何第三方共享数据。

你对数据的控制权

在"设置"中，你随时可以：

  • 导出我的数据 —— 生成包含你打卡与日记历史的文件。
  • 删除所有数据 —— 永久清除应用在本地和 iCloud 中的数据。

从设备卸载本应用会同时清除其本地存储；你可以提前通过应用内的删除功能清除 iCloud 数据，或在 iOS 设置中管理你的 iCloud 存储。

儿童隐私

Beads 不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会以不同于上述方式专门收集其数据（即完全不收集，仅限用户自己设备/iCloud 中的存储）。

政策变更

若本政策发生变更，上方的"最后更新"日期会相应更新。重大变更会在 App Store 页面中体现。
